👤 About

Hi!!! On this side of the screen is Sijan.

This started because I had way too much free time and didn’t know what to do with it, so I made this little space instead of letting my thoughts just disappear into scrolling.

I end up doing a bit of everything—writing, drawing, design, architecture, reading, programming. It doesn’t feel separate in my head. It all kind of blends together anyway.

Medicine and surgery are what I keep coming back to. It is a steady pull toward structure, precision, and understanding what lies beneath the surface of human systems. Something about it feels clear to me, even when other things don’t. I like understanding things—people, bodies, systems, thoughts. Sometimes I actually understand it. Sometimes I just think I do.

This isn’t anything serious or final. Just a small corner of the internet where I put things I’m thinking about. It will probably keep changing as I do. It is an ongoing notebook—part reflection, part experiment, part memory. Things here may be incomplete, revised, or contradicted later. That is intentional.

This is just a place where thoughts lingers instead of disappearing.
